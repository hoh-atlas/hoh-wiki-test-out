Leaderboard events are various types of small events that run for several days and challenge you with particular tasks to achieve milestones. By completing these tasks you receive trophies, that boost your position in the ranking of 100 randomly selected players.

Rally of Heroes

Rally of Heroes is a Leaderboard event that spans several days, challenging you to complete specific tasks to achieve milestones. Earning trophies boosts your position in the rankings, unlocking additional rewards that can be claimed once the event concludes.
